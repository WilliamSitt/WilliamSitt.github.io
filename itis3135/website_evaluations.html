Website Evaluations

LinkedIn

A business and employment-focused social media platform that works through websites and mobile apps. It allows it's members to manage their professional identity, build connections, and access knowledge, insights and opportunities.

  • Contrast: Good color contrast with black text and white background, only issue is when there is gray text with white background.
  • Repetition: Colors and fonts are consistent with most if not all elements.
  • Alignment: Elements are lined up and organized with neat visual connections.
  • Proximity: Related elements are group together and gaps between different elements are big enough.

Yale School of Art

A website for the Yale School of Art. It exists as an ongoing collaborative experiment in digital publishing and information sharing, and functions as a wiki, allowing members of the school to add new, and edit existing content.

  • Contrast: No issue with contrast between text and background colors.
  • Repetition: Not good, elements vary in colors and styling.
  • Alignment: Some alignment between elements, but not great.
  • Proximity: Gaps between unrelated elements are not big enough.
